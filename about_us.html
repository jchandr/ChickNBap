About Us

Chick-N-Bap was founded in 2013 by Sung Kim and Christian Ko who were inspired by the taste and experience of chicken over rice, a staple in the New York City street food scene. Due to their Korean heritage, they decided to integrate traditional Korean marinades and condiments to unify their love for NYC chicken over rice with Korean food, resulting in the creation of Chick-N-Bap.

With its current success, Chick-N-Bap aims introduce its delicious offering to many other college campuses around the country. If you would like to be a collegiate ambassador for Chick-N-Bap, please Contact Us Here
